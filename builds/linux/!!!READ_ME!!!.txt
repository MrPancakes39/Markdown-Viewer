After you extract the tar to where you want, edit the md-view.desktop file

- Edit the path to your markdown-viewer directory
- Copy or Move the md-view.desktop to /usr/share/applications/
    - to copy it just do "sudo cp md-view.desktop /usr/share/applications/md-view.desktop"
    - to move it just do "sudo mv md-view.desktop /usr/share/applications/"
